REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
Enterprise Software Solutions
RFP #2024-TECH-001

==================================================
SECTION 1: SECURITY & COMPLIANCE QUESTIONS
==================================================

1. What security certifications does your company hold? Please list all current certifications including SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and any others.

2. Describe your data encryption practices. How is data protected both at rest and in transit?

3. Does your platform support multi-factor authentication (MFA)? Please describe your authentication mechanisms.

4. Are you GDPR compliant? Please describe your data protection measures for European operations.

5. How often do you conduct penetration testing and security audits?

==================================================
SECTION 2: TECHNICAL INFRASTRUCTURE
==================================================

6. What is your guaranteed uptime SLA? What has been your actual uptime over the past 12 months?

7. Where are your data centers located? Do you have disaster recovery capabilities?

8. Describe your backup and recovery procedures. What are your RTO and RPO targets?

9. What API capabilities do you offer? Do you support REST and GraphQL?

10. What pre-built integrations are available (e.g., Salesforce, Jira, Slack)?

==================================================
SECTION 3: COMPANY & SUPPORT
==================================================

11. How many employees does your company have? How many are dedicated engineers?

12. What support options are available? Is 24/7 support included?

13. Do you provide a dedicated Customer Success Manager?

14. What is your typical implementation timeline for enterprise clients?

15. What training resources do you provide for new users?

==================================================
SECTION 4: PRICING & CONTRACTS
==================================================

16. Describe your pricing model. Is it subscription-based or one-time license?

17. What are your standard payment terms?

18. Do you offer volume discounts for large deployments?

==================================================
SECTION 5: EXPERIENCE & REFERENCES
==================================================

19. How many enterprise clients do you currently serve?
