# Sample RFP Questions Document

## Section 1: Company Information

### Question 1.1
Please describe your company's history and experience in this industry.

### Question 1.2
How many employees does your organization currently have?

### Question 1.3
Provide at least three client references from similar implementations.

## Section 2: Technical Requirements

### Question 2.1
Describe your system architecture and infrastructure.

### Question 2.2
What security certifications does your organization maintain?

### Question 2.3
How do you handle data backup and disaster recovery?

## Section 3: Implementation

### Question 3.1
What is your typical implementation timeline?

### Question 3.2
Describe your project management methodology.

### Question 3.3
What training and support do you provide during implementation?

## Section 4: Pricing

### Question 4.1
Provide detailed pricing for your proposed solution.

### Question 4.2
Are there any additional costs not included in the base price?

### Question 4.3
What are your payment terms?
